Short Bio

Executive Member of the Special Education Committee of the China Computer Federation (CCF), Member of the Special Committee on natural language generation and intelligent writing of CCF, Member of National Information Technology Standardization Technical Committee. I have won the first prize and the second prize of Beijing Education and teaching achievement award.

I am committed to scientific research and engineering project development in natural language processing, knowledge atlas, and document information processing. Moreover, I have presided over and participated in many national and provincial projects and published more than 30 SCI/EI indexed papers.
